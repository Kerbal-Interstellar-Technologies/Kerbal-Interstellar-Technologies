===Dependencies===
 - Deadly Reentry Continued plugin (NathanKell) - if installed, the Heat Shield will protect your entry devices from the heat and stresses of atmospheric reentry. 
   And will increase drag when open.

===How To Install===

1. Ensure that you have all of the aforementioned dependencies installed

2. Remove any previous version

3. Extract the .ZIP archive and copy the GameData folder provided into your KSP root directory

The parts should then be located under the OLDD/NASA folder
